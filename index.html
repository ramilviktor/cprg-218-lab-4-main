GORILLA

Click the button for a banana to give to the gorilla, to make the gorilla happy! Gorillas like bananas because they are a sweet and convenient source of energy. In the wild, gorillas primarily eat plants, fruits, and some insects. Bananas are easy for them to peel and eat, and they provide a quick source of carbohydrates, which are important for their energy needs. However, bananas are not a major part of a gorilla's natural diet, as they primarily eat a variety of plants and fruits based on what is available in their habitat.
